My central question I would like to ask, is: Whose body is performing the script? I would like to explain this with a bit more context but I also want to disclaim that I am at the start of this thesis and there might be some flaws or things that doesn’t make sense yet. My inquiry relates to the previously explained technological conditions of human/user in relation to machines and their scripted influence. Im this question I use „body“ as a non specific term to address the user and to open the question into two directions. First it could relate to the identity of WHO is the person. Is it the SAME person as the performer? And secondly you can understand it as a possessive form – to WHOM does the body belong that is performing the script? Is it THEIR body performing, or is it the choreographer’s vision/intent coming through them? All in all it raises questions of agency and influence, authorship and autonomy. I would like to draw a conceptual comparison with the characteristics of a performative practices of theatre plays where performances are considered as a laid-out instruction and a twice behaved or stored and recalled (embodied) behaviour executed by a body of a performer who is behaving as someone else, as another „me“. With my thesis I want to make the assertion that in the relationship between humans and machines, the users are performing actors, playing a role and are controlled by the screenplay, the script of the programmer as dramaturge.In other words computation dictates who’s role we are playing and how we “behave.” Based on this analogy, I would like to reflect on the opportunities to escape/refuse this rigid environment in order to gain more agency by asking: Does the abstractness of a (software) script hold the possibility of misinterpretation? Where are moments of impulsiveness in improvisation/off-script/spontaneity/free-style? Where and who is the audience? Who is the protagonist and who is the supporting cast? Is there a possibility of collaboration between user-performer and programmer-dramaturg? Who is the voice inside the prompter? And Who is the stunt-double?
